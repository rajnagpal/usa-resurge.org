Resurge Ingredients

Resurge boasts a carefully curated blend of natural ingredients, each selected for its unique health benefits. This supplement's formula is a testament to the power of nature, combining essential vitamins, minerals, and herbs known to enhance metabolism, improve sleep, and support overall well-being. From minerals like magnesium and zinc to herbs such as ashwagandha and melatonin, each ingredient plays a pivotal role in ensuring that Resurge delivers on its promise of holistic health enhancement.

Here is a list of major Resurge Vitamins & Ingredients along with their benefits: 

1

Magnesium

**Magnesium** is a vital mineral that plays a role in over 300 enzymatic reactions within the body. It's essential for energy production, muscle and nerve function, and maintaining a regular heartbeat. Additionally, magnesium helps regulate blood sugar levels and supports the body's immune system.
2

Zinc

**Zinc** is an essential trace element that is crucial for numerous aspects of cellular metabolism. It plays a role in immune function, protein synthesis, wound healing, and DNA synthesis. Furthermore, zinc is vital for proper growth and development during pregnancy, childhood, and adolescence.
3

Melatonin

**Melatonin** is a hormone produced by the pineal gland in the brain. It's primarily known for its role in regulating sleep-wake cycles. Melatonin release is influenced by the day-night cycle, helping to synchronize our internal body clock with the external environment.
4

L-Theanine

**L-Theanine** is an amino acid commonly found in tea leaves. It is known for its calming effects on the brain, promoting relaxation without drowsiness. L-Theanine can help reduce stress and improve focus and attention.
5

Ashwagandha

**Ashwagandha** is a traditional herb used in Ayurvedic medicine. It's classified as an adaptogen, meaning it can help the body manage stress. Ashwagandha also provides numerous other benefits for the body and brain, such as reducing blood sugar levels, reducing cortisol, boosting brain function, and helping fight symptoms of anxiety and depression.
6

Hydroxytryptophan

**Hydroxytryptophan** is a naturally occurring amino acid and a precursor to the neurotransmitter serotonin. It plays a significant role in mood regulation, sleep, and appetite. Supplementing with hydroxytryptophan can help improve mood, reduce symptoms of depression, and enhance sleep quality.
7

Lysine

**Lysine** is an essential amino acid that plays a crucial role in protein synthesis, hormone production, and the absorption of calcium. It's also vital for the production of collagen, a protein that helps maintain the structure of skin, tendons, and bones.
8

Arginine

**Arginine** is an amino acid that plays a role in building protein. It also gets converted into nitric oxide in the body, which causes blood vessels to open wider for improved blood flow. Arginine also stimulates the release of growth hormone and insulin, aiding in muscle metabolism.
